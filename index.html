Toronto Overwatch Beer League

Toronto's first social Overwatch LAN gaming league

Team registration is now open!

How it works

Create a team or sign up as an individual and get drafted.

Compete weekly in an organized Overwatch league. See the details here.

Play on LAN at Raiders eSports Centre. Have fun and socialize.

The first season begins in February, and individual registration opens soon, so sign up to receive updates!

Raiders eSports Centre

Raiders boasts 7000 square feet, 21 high end gaming PCs, 30 TVs, retro gaming machines, and a full service kitchen. Located just two minutes from St. Clair Station.
